Flushing Food Tour

Suggested Itinerary

1

Appetizers: Start off light by grabbing savory Jianbing from Eight Jane or steamed buns from Yuan Wei.

2

Relax & Digest: Walk to Amazing Time Beauty to completely unwind with a Head Spa or massage while your appetizers settle.

3

The Main Event: Head over to Xi'an Famous Foods, Chopin Noodle House, or Henan Feng Wei for hand-ripped noodles, or grab some amazing dumplings at Zhu Ji Dumpling House.

4

Sweet Finish: Conclude the trip with a silky tofu pudding at Soy Bean Chen Flower Shop, grab some incredible egg tarts from Na Tart, or get the best brown sugar boba at Xing Fu Tang.
